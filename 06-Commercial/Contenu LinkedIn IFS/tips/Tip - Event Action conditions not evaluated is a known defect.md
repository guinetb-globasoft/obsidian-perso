💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

Event Action conditions that **"sometimes" don't evaluate**? Before you redesign your events — it was a known defect, not your logic.

• Fixed in Apps 10 UPD20
• On IFS Cloud: 22R2 SU14 / 23R1 SU7 / 23R2 GA / 24R1 `[à vérifier]`

If you're on an older SU, the fix is to **patch up**, not to rebuild your rules around a phantom bug. The wider habit worth keeping: **intermittent** behaviour usually means *defect*, not *design* — check the known-issues / SU notes before assuming your configuration is at fault.
