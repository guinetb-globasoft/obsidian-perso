💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

If copy/paste on posting lines vanished for your finance team, you're not imagining it — it was deliberately disabled on transactional pages to prevent data corruption.

• It returns in **26R1** (not back-ported to earlier Cloud) `[à vérifier]`
• Scope: posting lines in Posting Proposal, Manual Supplier Invoice, Manage Supplier Invoice

So this is a **version target, not a workaround to engineer**. If month-end leans on it, put it in the upgrade business case rather than building a fragile alternative. Knowing what's coming in a release is as valuable as knowing what broke.
