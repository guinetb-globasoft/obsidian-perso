💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

Need MWO resource **break** and **On/Off-shift** data but can't find where it lives? It's stored in the **ResourceLogonStatusHist** entity.

• Point **Query Designer** at it to surface logon/logoff and shift history
• From there you can report on actual time on/off shift per resource

A lot of "IFS doesn't capture that" turns out to be "it's in an entity nobody mentioned." When data looks missing, search the entities before concluding it isn't tracked.
