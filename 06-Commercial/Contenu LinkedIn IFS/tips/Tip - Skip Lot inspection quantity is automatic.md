💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

Trying to hard-set the inspection quantity on a Skip Lot / acceptance-sampling control plan? You can't — and that's the whole point of the feature.

• The quantity to inspect is **calculated** by the sampling logic, per the standard you chose
• Good quality over time → **fewer** inspections; quality slips → **more**

So if you want to inspect less, you don't override the number — you **tune the sampling standard**. When a feature won't let you set a value manually, it's usually telling you that value is supposed to be earned, not entered.
