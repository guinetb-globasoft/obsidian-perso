💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

In **Int Purch Direct / Int Purch Trans** intercompany flows, no Purchase Requisition gets created — and that's **by design**, not a missing step.

• Internal Supplier + Internal Customer = a firm, pre-agreed internal trade
• The supplying site is already known, so IFS skips the "request" stage and goes straight to the order on release

Before debugging a "missing PR," confirm whether the flow is intercompany. The requisition legitimately disappears there — chasing it is time you won't get back.
