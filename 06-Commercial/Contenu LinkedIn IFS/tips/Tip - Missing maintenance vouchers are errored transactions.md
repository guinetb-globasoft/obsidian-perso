**Pilier** : Service / Maintenance

💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

Maintenance transactions not turning into vouchers? Before you suspect the posting setup, look for **errored transactions**.

The Maintenance Voucher Transfer jobs (Transfer Maintenance Time, Tools & Facilities, etc.) **silently skip** any transaction in error — so it just never posts, with no obvious warning.

• Find and fix the errored transactions
• Re-run the transfer

A "missing voucher" in IFS is almost always a blocked transaction upstream, not a broken posting rule. Check the data before the config.
