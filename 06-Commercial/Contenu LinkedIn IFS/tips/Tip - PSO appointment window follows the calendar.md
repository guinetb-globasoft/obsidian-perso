💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

Wondering what really controls the appointment-window horizon in PSO (Planning & Scheduling Optimization)? It's your **working-day calendar** — not a hidden PSO parameter.

• Move the calendar from 5 to 7 working days and the horizon stretches accordingly (e.g. to ~42 days)
• So "no available slots" is often a **calendar definition**, not an optimisation limit

Before tuning PSO settings, check the calendar driving the horizon. In scheduling, the real constraint is usually one layer upstream of where everyone looks first.
