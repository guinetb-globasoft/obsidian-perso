**Pilier** : Service Management

💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

SLA commitments not being created on your requests? A classic cause: the **SLA template is missing its Fulfillment Entity**.

• Diff a working template against the broken one
• The one that fails usually has no Fulfillment Entity set

SLAs fail **quietly** — no error, just no commitment generated. When one template works and another doesn't, compare them field by field; the gap is almost always a missing mandatory link, not a bug.
