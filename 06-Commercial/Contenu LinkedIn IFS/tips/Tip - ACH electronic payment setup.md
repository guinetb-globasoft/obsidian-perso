💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

Setting up ACH payments feels daunting — until you realise every electronic payment file in IFS follows the *same* skeleton. Only the country/format-specific data changes.

The pattern:
• Enable the **payment format** (per company)
• Create the **payment method**
• Enable that method for the **payment institution**
• Adjust the **supplier setup** for the format

Learn it once and BACS, SEPA, ACH, ISO 20022 stop being separate projects and become variations on a theme. Half of IFS finance setup is recognising the pattern under the country quirks.
