**Pilier** : Custom Fields & Workflow

💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

A custom field (enumeration) won't take its default value and you're deep in Page Designer? Step back — it's usually a **lifecycle-state** problem, not a layout one.

Check, in order:
• Is the **enumeration** Approved, Published **and** Synchronised?
• Is the **custom field** itself approved?

An unpublished enum silently refuses to default. In IFS configuration, "it doesn't work" very often means "it isn't published yet." Verify states before you debug behaviour — it's the fastest fix you'll never see coming.
