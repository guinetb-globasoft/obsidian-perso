**Pilier** : Asset Management

💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

Need to turn a **Company Rental Asset** into a **Company Owned** one? There's a built-in action — no data surgery, no script.

• Open the part in **Inventory Part in Stock**
• Check the **Ownership Type** (shows "Company Rental Asset")
• From the line/command actions, choose **Transfer Company Rental Asset to Company Owned**

When an ownership or status change feels like it needs a back-end fix, look for the dedicated action first. IFS usually ships one — and using it keeps the audit trail clean.
