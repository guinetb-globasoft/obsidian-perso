💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

"The forward/back navigation arrows disappeared in Aurena!" — almost always **not** a bug.

• The arrows toggle with **screen / viewport size**
• At full desktop width they reappear

Before logging a ticket, resize the window or go full screen. A real share of "missing UI" reports in Aurena are **responsive-layout** behaviour, not defects — knowing that saves a support round-trip and a frustrated user. When something vanishes at narrow widths, suspect the layout before the software.
