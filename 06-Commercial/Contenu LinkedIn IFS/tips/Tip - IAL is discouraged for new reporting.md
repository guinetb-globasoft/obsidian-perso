💡 **IFS Cloud tip**

Still building new reports on **IAL** (Information Access Layer)? There's no deprecation date yet — but IFS now **actively discourages** new IAL usage in its tech docs.

• Prefer the newer access options (information sources / projections / analysis models)
• Reserve IAL for the rare case where genuinely nothing else fits

Building net-new on a "discouraged" mechanism is future upgrade debt you'll pay later. Check the recommended option **before** reaching for IAL out of habit.
